How to Reach VIT Vellore

By Bus

  • Vellore New Bus Stand (New Bus Terminus)
  • Buses from Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad
  • Campus distance: ~7 km (autos/taxis available)
  • Nearby stops: VIT Bus Stop, Katpadi

By Train

  • Katpadi Junction (KPD)
  • Well-connected to major cities
  • Short auto/taxi ride to campus

By Air

  • Chennai International Airport (MAA) (~125–130 km)
  • Taxi/bus/train from Chennai
  • Alternatives: Tirupati (TIR), Bengaluru (BLR)

Places to Visit in Vellore

Discover top attractions for history, spirituality, nature & culture around Vellore.

Vellore Fort

Historic fort with expansive ramparts and an onsite museum showcasing ancient relics.

Sri Lakshmi Narayani Golden Temple

Jalakandeswarar Temple

Ratnagiri Balamurugan Temple
