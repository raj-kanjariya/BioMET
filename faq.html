Frequently Asked Questions

Biomet Conference – FAQs

Find answers related to submissions, registration, publication, venue, and program details.

What is the deadline for abstract submission?

Deadline for abstract submission is 15th April 2026.

Where can I find the abstract submission link and template?

Use the following link for abstract submission and template download:
https://vit.ac.in/conference/biomet-2026/abstracts.html

What are the accepted presentation categories and formats?

Oral and Poster presentation categories are accepted.

Can I submit a Review Article?

No. Review articles are not accepted. Only original research papers are considered.

Are team presentations allowed?

No. Team presentations are not allowed. Individual presentations only.

When does registration open, and what is the procedure?

Abstract submission opens on 15th January 2026 and closes on 15th April 2026.
Registration can be completed via:
https://vit.ac.in/conference/biomet-2026/registration.html

What are the registration fees? Are there specific rates for students?

Please refer to the registration page for complete fee structure and student rates: View Registration Fees

Is participation allowed for non-presenters (attendees only)?

Yes, participation is allowed for non-presenters.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, certificates will be provided to participants.

Will accepted papers be published in indexed journals?

Yes, selected papers will be published in a Scopus-indexed journal as part of a special issue. Final acceptance is subject to the journal’s peer-review process.

Are there any Article Processing Charges (APC)?

APC details will depend on the selected journal and will be communicated to authors during publication. For clarification, contact biomet2026@gmail.com.

Is accommodation available on campus?

Accommodation may be available on campus depending on availability. Please contact biomet2026@gmail.com for details.

How do I reach the venue?

Travel details and directions are available at: Venue & Travel Information

Who are the keynote speakers?

Speaker details are available at: Conference Homepage

How can I apply to give an invited talk?

Send your request with CV and topic details to biomet2026@gmail.com.

For publishing, logistics, or general inquiries, contact:

Email: biomet2026@gmail.com
